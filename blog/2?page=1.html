Birthday "cupcakes"

August 3, 2012 - 10:43am — Emily Whebbe

I searched high and low for a good recipe that I felt comfortable feeding Reverie for her first birthday. I wanted to avoid sugar, grains, oils, eggs, and dairy. Usually, cupcakes are kind of a difficult thing to make without any of the above. Then I remembered coconut flour. It's a great non-grain to use and full of fiber and protein. I substitute it in many of my recipes calling for whole wheat flour (as they have similar "grainy" texture), but the only quip is that it requires a lot of eggs or egg-like foods to bind it together as it doesn't have the glutenous properties grain flours have.
