Banana Cream Pie (an EW original)

July 13, 2012 - 3:50pm — Emily Whebbe

I think banana cream pie is one of my favorite desserts. I don't make it nearly often enough, probably because I don't make enough time to make pie crust (because I like it best with a nice buttery pastry crust). But, I decided to make graham cracker crust this time, and it turned out nearly as well. This is an Emily Whebbe original, and is thus, doesn't include many amounts of anything.

Crust:

Graham crackers (ground in the blender)
Butter (melted and cooled)

I have no idea how much of each of these I used, but you'll figure it out.

Filling:

Vanilla pudding (I used the organic mix from the co-op because it saves time but you could make your own easily too.)
3 tbs chia seeds
cinnamon
cloves
2-3 bananas (1 mashed, 1-2 sliced)
blueberries

Directions:

Melt the butter (or coconut oil) and combine with crushed graham crackers. Press into pie pan. Prepare vanilla putting. While cooling, add chia seeds and 1 mashed banana. While pudding thickens, line pan with sliced bananas. When pudding is cooled, pour over sliced bananas and top with blueberries. Place in refrigerator for a few hours to set. Eat. Yum.
