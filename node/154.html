Buckwheat Muffins

August 1, 2011 - 7:22pm — Emily Whebbe

I found this recipe proclaiming to be wheat, dairy, and sugar free. While the muffins it creates are all of these things they aren't that appealing as such, so I've modified it a bit. To undo my modifications, forget the honey/sugar.

1/2 cup ground flax seeds (flax meal)
1 1/2 cups buckwheat flour
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg
pinch of cloves
2 eggs
1 cup almond milk
4 apples (peeled and grated)
1 banana
6 tablespoons coconut oil
1/4 to 1/2 cup honey or sweetener of choice

Combine the dry ingredients and wet ingredients separately, then add together, being careful not to over-mix the batter. Bake at 350 degrees for about 20 minutes.

If you like, top warm muffins with a little coconut oil and honey.
